     (rombar=0 — no ROM BAR, avoid VBIOS issues with laptop variant)
     (no x-vga — not primary display)

Key Lessons — Laptop NVIDIA GPU Passthrough:
  - disable_vga=1 prevents host from trying to use GPU as VGA
  - disable_idle_d3=1 prevents laptop power management from putting GPU to sleep
  - video=efifb:off prevents efifb from claiming the GPU framebuffer
  - rombar=0 avoids VBIOS ROM issues (laptop VBIOSes are often problematic)
  - No x-vga flag needed (use serial console instead)
  - Driver version 580 from Ubuntu repos (script targeted 550, repo had 580)
